Language exchange apps

by Arnis

InterPals more info

One of the most distinctive features of InterPals is its focus on language exchange. The app allows users to search for language partners with whom they can practice speaking, writing, and reading in a foreign language. This is particularly useful for people who are learning a new language and need someone to practice with. Users can search for partners based on their interests, age, location, and proficiency level in the target language. This way, they can find someone who is a good match for them and start practicing right away.

Tandem more info

The app allows users to communicate with their language partners through text, voice, and video calls. This makes it easy for users to practice speaking, listening, and writing in a foreign language with native speakers. Users can also receive feedback from their language partners on their grammar, pronunciation, and vocabulary.
