LibriVox more info

LibriVox is a non-profit organization that provides free audiobooks of public domain works. The project is built on the principles of open access and collaborative effort. LibriVox volunteers select public domain books, which include classic literature, historical texts, and other works whose copyrights have expired. Volunteers then record themselves reading the selected texts, typically one chapter or section at a time. These recordings are carefully edited and compiled to create complete audiobooks.

Blinkist more info

Blinkist is a popular subscription-based digital platform that provides summaries of non-fiction books in bite-sized formats. It was founded in 2012 by Holger Seim, Niklas Jansen, Tobias Balling, Sebastian Klein, and Matthias Klein. The platform aims to help individuals consume key insights and knowledge from a wide range of books in a concise and easily digestible manner.
